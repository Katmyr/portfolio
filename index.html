About me

I am a UX Design student with a particular focus on technical environments and complex systems. My main goal is to give time back to the user. By analyzing real-world usage and production constraints, I structure information and user flows to make them clearer, smoother, and more efficient.

For the user, this means less hesitation, fewer errors, and a reduced cognitive load.
For the company, it leads to better tool adoption, optimized processes, and decisions backed by a deep understanding of field needs.

I am currently looking for an internship to bring this rigor to a team, learn alongside multidisciplinary profiles, and contribute to projects where every interaction has a concrete impact.
